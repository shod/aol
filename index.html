AGENCY ONLINE

A business platform for lighting sales agencies.

What is Agency Online?

Agency Online is a front-end application designed specifically for lighting sales agencies to consolidate, distribute, report, and act on all customer and project information, from pre-design through to warranty. Whether on mobile, tablet, or laptop, information is available in real time to whoever needs it, whenever they need it, wherever they are.

With Agency Online, you'll have a structured sales process and a central communication and collaboration center. Your entire organization – from bid team to project lead to management – will interact on the same platform ensuring that everyone has access to the information they need. No longer will critical information be hidden in inaccessible e-mail accounts or unavailable spreadsheets.

With Agency Online, you'll view, measure, and report on manufacturer and customer performance including analytics on product mixes and manufacturer mix by customer. You'll have visibility into the sales pipeline with a clear understanding of the orders that are booked in, held for release, or released in production. Instant access to enterprise-wide orders will provide you with leverage when negotiating prices with manufacturers or distributors.

Although designed for lighting sales agencies, Agency Online is not “off-the-shelf” software – it's custom-designed for your specific needs. Our team of analysts and technicians will work with you to create a software solution as unique as your business.

THE BUSINESS CASE

Lighting sales agencies face many challenges:

  • Disconnected Communication – Critical information is hidden in e-mail messages that aren't accessible to those who need it.
  • Inability to Adjust Quote at the Last Moment – Scattered information prevents rapid quote adjustments.
  • Lots of Data, but Little Information – Although project and customer data exists, it's isolated and cannot quickly be converted to actionable information.
  • Limited Support of Mobile Force – Data and information is unavailable to mobile users which slows sales and delays decisions.
  • Lack of Visibility into the Sales Pipeline – Inability to view bids and orders across projects, customers, and manufacturers prevents using economies of scale to lower prices.
  • Managerial Bottleneck – Expanding and training the sales team requires excessive effort by management which restricts the ability to scale operations.

Agency Online provides a structured sales process where all information is centralized and accessible across your organization. A simple, intuitive interface, along with targeted notifications and real-time data, provides access to critical information anywhere, anytime, and on any device.
  • Effective Communication – Team members have access to all critical
  • Rapid Quote Adjustment – Adjusting a quote to a contractor with the greatest likelihood of getting a project increases your odds of a successful bid.
  • Effective Reporting – Agency Online integrates data from multiple sources to provide a comprehensive but simple view of your business.
  • Full Mobile Access – Team members can work as effectively in the field as they do in the office.
  • Sales Pipeline Clarity – Simplified reporting allows you to visualize critical business metrics at a glance with the ability to drill down to specific details.
  • Streamlined Management – Valuable time previously spent on status meetings can now be spent on value-added activities. Additional sales staff are more easily brought on-board since they can see at-a-glance your sales process and project work-flow.

Improvements in communication, reporting, and processes are only worthwhile if they also improve bottom-line results. An average-sized lighting agency has:
  • $100-125M per year in quotes.
  • $30-55M per year in sales (based on a 30-50% conversion rate).
With the improvements Agency Online can make to your sales process, project management, and reporting, a 2-5% increase in your conversion rate will result in addition revenue of:
  • Additional revenue: $330K - $2.75M
